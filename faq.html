Welcome to the FAQ section


Q: Are PCs better than consoles?

A: Absolutely yes, PCs have squillions more games, are cheaper and more powerful if specced out right, can do more things and you don't have to pay to play online games besides internet.

Q: Why go for 60+ fps when the eye can only see 30?

A: PC players try to achieve 60 fps because the human eye can see above 30. The eye not being able to see above 30fps is just a straight-up lie and can be proved so with a simple google or test.

Q: Is keyboard and mouse better than a controller?

A: Yes and no, keyboard and mouse is far superior to the controller in FPS and MOBA games and better in the other categories as well but the controller has the leg up in racing games due to its analogue movement.

Q: Should I buy Razer products?

A: If you aren't a millionaire then you shouldn't, not only do Razer products cost a lot but they are also prone to breaking, on forums, there is always someone complaining about how their razer products broke after only a year despite what the five-star reviews say from sponsored people who haven't used the product for more than a week.
